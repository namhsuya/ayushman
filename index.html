Ayushman Kumar Banerjee

I am currently a junior computational biologist at Bencos Research Solutions

I completed my undergraduate studies in biotechnology at Amity University, Kolkata in 2020, the year of the coronavirus pandemic.

I worked under Dr Chittabrata Mal in studying and developing a network analysis based methodology to identify functional miRNA clusters responsible for regulation of rice metabolic pathways. With an academic background in life sciences and my inherent interest in IT, I am constantly working towards creating a right mix of both these domains to observe, examine and possibly solve biological problems.

Presently, my major focus at work is to deal with NGS data analysis work which mostly revolves around RNA-Seq and Metagenomic data. I am also interested in clinical applications of deep learning.
